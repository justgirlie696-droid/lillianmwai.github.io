Research & Publications

Assessment of Micro Financial Institution Funding on the Performance of Small and Medium-Sized Enterprises in Muranga County

Academic Research Microfinance SME Development

This comprehensive study examines the relationship between microfinance funding and the growth metrics of small and medium-sized enterprises in Muranga County, Kenya. The research employs mixed-methods approaches, combining quantitative analysis of financial data with qualitative interviews with SME owners to provide nuanced insights into funding accessibility and its impact on business sustainability.

Determinants of Access to Credit from Micro Finance Institutions: A Case Study of Nairobi CBD

Academic Research Credit Access Financial Inclusion

An in-depth analysis of factors influencing credit accessibility from microfinance institutions in Nairobi's Central Business District. The study identifies key determinants including business documentation, credit history, and demographic factors, proposing a framework for enhancing financial inclusion among urban small business owners through targeted policy interventions and financial literacy programs.

PhD Research: Financial Strategies for Institutional Sustainability

Doctoral Dissertation In Progress Financial Modeling

My ongoing doctoral research focuses on developing innovative financial models to enhance long-term institutional viability and growth. The study examines case studies from both for-profit and non-profit organizations, analyzing how strategic financial planning, resource allocation, and risk management contribute to sustainable organizational development in dynamic economic environments.

Integrating Industry Practice into Business Education: A Framework for Enhanced Student Employability

This pedagogical study proposes a comprehensive framework for integrating real-world industry practices into business education curricula.
